What is BANANAA ?

BANANAA is a simple food suggestion portal that helps people decide what to order at a restaurant, based on people's recommendations.

Why we do it?

We believe that every guest, visiting a restaurant, deserves the best food. And every restaurant wants to serve his customer the best food - Always!


However, at times a customer feels unsatisfied because he did not like what he ordered. We believe, a small effort in helping him decide what he should order would go a long way in making him happy and satisfied.


This is an initiative to reduce the amount of unhappy customers.

How do we do it?

We engage people on our platform and ask them one simple question - What do they love eating at a restaurant? People can recommend the food or drinks that they love and by making use of programming power, we come up with food suggestions for that restaurant based entirely on what people recommend.


It is important to notice that this is not a review platform and therefore we do not ask a person what he doesn't recommend. Thereby removing the scope for forged, biased or negative reviews.






How does it benefit you?

We believe the biggest business benefit of BANANAA for a restaurant is their happy customers. And this happiness is driven by themselves as they recommend for each other, the food they love.


Food is the core offering for any restaurant and Bananaa helps in giving the visibility to it. It lets people recommend, collaborate and talk about specific dishes served at a restaurant.
