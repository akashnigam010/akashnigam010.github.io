Food suggestions is our game and we are committed to bring you the best.


Adding your foodviews and ratings on Bananaa is open to all and everyone is invited to do so. However, before we add a restaurant on Bananaa, our team does a pretty decent job of conducting a small research for it.


We go over all the possible social media platforms (you name it), collect suggestions from people, conduct personal interviews and come up with a list of most recommended food at a restaurant, before we open it to the community to refine the results.


That way, we do our homework before enlisting a restaurant on Bananaa and make sure you never go wrong with your order.



And what happens after that ?


Well, after that its you and your friend's foodviews and ratings, that keep changing the shape of it.
So go ahead, be a part of the community and recommend the food you love and help us in making this world a better place to eat.

Eat all the good food - BANANAA!
